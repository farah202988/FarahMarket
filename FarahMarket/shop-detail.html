Tunisian baklewa is a rich, layered pastry made with thin sheets of dough, filled with a mixture of crushed nuts like almonds or pistachios, and sweetened with fragrant syrup or honey. Its delicate texture and blend of flavors make it a beloved treat in Tunisian cuisine.
